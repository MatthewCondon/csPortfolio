Raise The Bar!


I made this game last year after I concluded my Clock Project. The aim of this game is to tap your mouse as fast as possible and (1) Make the L switch to a W by getting to the end first and (2) Get to the end so in the lowest time possible. The high score is 6 seconds for the win.

The most challenging part about this project was making the bar move in a consistent motion. In the initial stages, the bar would appear at a distance, disappear, and reappear at a new distance. Other than that, it was not too difficult to code. Feel free to change the frameCount variable to make the computer move its bar faster or slower. Have fun!
