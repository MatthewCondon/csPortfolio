Scary Halloween

This was an attempt at a scaled project. However, I got too far in with hard code before realizing where I made an error –– the faces had specific designs. To do the math to figure out where toput pupils, teeth, mouths, and other things would have been extremely difficult.

The hardest part was putting those items where they are right now. It was just extremely specific where they needed to be.

You may also see the light that flickers on the inside of the pumpkin. This was pretty fun to do and even more satisfying to watch. It is also on a timer that switches every three seconds.

Want to see the code? Here's the link: Jack O' Lantern Code

I hope you are inspired to carve your own pumpkins this Halloween with some of my designs!
