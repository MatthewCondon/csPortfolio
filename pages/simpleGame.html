Chase The Tie Fighters!

This was a "simple" game that we made as a sort of capstone project for trimester one of Advanced Computer Science. For this, I made Space Invaders with a twist.

The aim of the game is to chase down the Tie Fighters in your X-Wing before they get away. You want to score above 100 in order to do it successfully.

There were two primary challenges. The first was figuring out the logic when Tie Fighters are shot. I solved this issue using various booleans and methods so determine what they should look like at any given momnet. The other difficult part was inputting sound features. It was mainly because I did not have much experience with using sounds in my code.

If you want to change the "planet," you can do that by going to draw() and changing the parameters being called in setPlanet(). To play, there are three main instructions. First, to get everything situated once it starts running, quickly press the mouse on the screen. Then, to move the X-Wing back and forth, use the left adn right arrow keys (note - going too far over on one side will make you reappear on the other). Finally, to shoot, press the spacebar. Regardless of whether you win or lose, the final sound will be sure to amuse you.
