The Model Of Our Solar System


This is a project that I made in my spare time to start this school year. I wanted to try something challenging and this certainly was. The most difficult part was getting the planets to move at different speeds on different orbits. It was also challenging adding the stars in the background. Despite the issues encountered, this was very enjoyable to work on. I hope you like it!
